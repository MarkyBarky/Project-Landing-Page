Koalas are awesome, fluffy and cuddly

This website has some nice pictures of happy koalas. The term "Koala" comes from an Aboriginal word and their main habitats are open forests and woodlands of Australia. They smell like eucalyptus leaves and can sleep up to 18 hours a day!
